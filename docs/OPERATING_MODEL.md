# Enablement Operating Model

## Team topology

| Role | Accountability |
|---|---|
| Enablement Lead | Portfolio strategy, benefits, risk, executive reporting |
| Automation Engineer | Workflow implementation, integration, reliability |
| Operational Analyst | Process mapping, baselines, KPI integrity |
| Adoption Partner | Change plan, training, feedback, usage tracking |
| Delivery Champion | Domain acceptance, exceptions, local adoption |

## Lean service model

- **Tier 0:** self-service templates, documentation, and automated validation.
- **Tier 1:** enablement office hours for configuration and adoption support.
- **Tier 2:** engineering support for integrations and complex failure recovery.
- **Tier 3:** platform escalation for security, architecture, or systemic incidents.

## Portfolio cadence

- Daily: automated health and SLA monitoring.
- Weekly: failure review, blocked adoption, and capacity allocation.
- Monthly: value, quality, cost, and service performance review.
- Quarterly: roadmap reprioritisation and benefits-realisation audit.

## Definition of done

A workflow is production-ready only when ownership, tests, monitoring, security review,
rollback, documentation, KPI baseline, service level, and adoption plan are complete.
